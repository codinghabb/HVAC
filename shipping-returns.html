Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping Information

At Reliable HVAC Parts, we strive to provide the fastest and most affordable shipping options for all our customers. We offer various shipping methods to suit your needs, ensuring your HVAC parts arrive on time and in perfect condition.

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: Delivered within 5-7 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Delivered within 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Delivered the next business day for urgent orders.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

Shipping Costs

  • Standard Shipping: $5.99 for orders under $50, free for orders over $50.
  • Expedited Shipping: $15.00 for all orders.
  • Overnight Shipping: $30.00 for all orders.

International Shipping

We currently only offer shipping to the United States and Canada. International shipping is not available at this time.

Return & Exchange Policy

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help. We offer a hassle-free return policy to ensure you're fully satisfied with your purchase.

Returns

You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging. To start a return, please contact our customer service team.

Non-returnable Items

  • Items that have been installed or used.
  • Items without the original packaging or labels.
  • Special-order or custom-made items.

How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, follow these simple steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@reliablehvacparts.com to request a return authorization number.
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
  3. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping service (we recommend using USPS, UPS, or FedEx).
  4. Once we receive your return, we will process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item, please follow the same procedure as a return and place a new order for the replacement item once the original item is returned.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method once your return is approved. Please note that it may take 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account depending on your payment provider.
